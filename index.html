About Me

Hello and welcome! My name is Nadia and I am a Front-End Web Developer based in the Greater Toronto Area. My interest in ocean life led me to study Marine Biology, my passion for teaching led me to a career in education and my love for logic and problem solving led me into the ever-evolving world of coding.

All the skills I’ve picked up from my past lives have strengthened my ability to be adaptable, collaborative and creative. I value hard work and integrity. This core belief can be reflected in my work; I strive to create accessible, responsive, user-friendly websites and applications using clean, organized code. I love learning new things and look forward to refining my skills with an exciting team of passionate people.

When I’m not coding and learning new skills, you can find me reading a book (print only!), planning my next travel adventure, cooking, watching baking shows or creating personalized cards for loved ones.

But enough about me - I’d love to hear from you! If you’d like to connect with me, or share any travel adventure stories, please fill out the contact form below.
